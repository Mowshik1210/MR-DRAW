02 — HOW IT WORKS

DRAWING IN AIR IS
AS EASY AS POINTING.

01

Allow Camera

We request 1280x720 @ 60fps. Only your hand is processed, background stays #000.

getUserMedia({video:{facingMode:'user'}})
02

Show Hand

HandLandmarker detects 21 landmarks, smoothed by LandmarkSmoother (One-Euro). Mask built from connections + palm polygon.

Landmarker + OneEuroFilter
03

Pinch / Point to Draw

Pinch distance = thumb(4)-index(8). Ratio = screenDist/palmSize. Point = index extended, others folded. 2 frames to start.

ratio <0.42 = DRAW
04

Lift to New Line

Open hand ✋ or Fist ✊ or 4 frames without pinch = instant lift. Current stroke committed to persistent canvases.

openFrames >=4 → endStroke()
05

Move Art in Space

Enable MOVE mode, pinch near art and drag. translateAll(dx,dy) updates all points and _redrawAll() re-renders glow+core.

translateAll(dx,dy)

This project started as a question: what if the hand is the only light in a pure black space? No UI clutter, no background, just skin tone floating in void with cinematic glow.

V1 was isolation only. V2 became a full air-drawing studio after users asked for persistent neon, proper lift gesture, and the ability to physically move drawings in space. The goal was to feel like Minority Report but with neon ink that never fades.
